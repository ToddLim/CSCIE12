Basics

Okay, so every so often, you'll want to administer a bath and haricut for your dogs. Depending on breed, size, and type of fur/hair, your cost will vary across different businesses. If you don't mind paying a little more for a brand you can trust, I would recommend seeing PetCo for its bathing and grooming packages. You can simply ask one of the employees to take care of your dog's business. The median sales price typically hovers around $60 USD.

Advanced

If you're looking for a more personal approach for your animal companions, you can try bathing and grooming them yourself--it might even be cheaper for you if you know exactly what you're doing! Check out this video for more about the materials and steps.
