Introduction To Hygiene For Dogs

Okay, okay. This is probably one of the most difficult responsibilities about having companionship with dogs. However, proper care in brushing their teeth, cleaning up after their bodily waste, and de-scentifying their beds can go a long way towards not just your dog's cleanliness, but your cleanliness as well.
